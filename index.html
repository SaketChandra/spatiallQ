Anamorph as a tool for interactive non-rigid transformation

About

Anamorphism is a reflective perception, a mathematical transformation to deform image. Actual image can be seen only in respective catoptric reflective mirror from a particular vantage point

Why Puzzle?

It's accepted in research community tha jigsaw puzzle is benefitial for Short term memory retention, Pattern fitting. Analytical Planning whch is good enough to guage cognitive abilities. Its shown that it increases children's spatial abilties too.


Actual image in center and anamorphism based image in surrounding.

Non-rigid transformation

It's quiet evident that anamorphism involves non-rigid transformations which is controlled based on surface gradient and location of viewer.

Hypothesis

Anamorph bases puzzle helps improve non-rigid tranformation skills

Methodology

After design of puzzle for test suite design was planned. User is first made aware about concepts of reflection through demonstration of transformations involving basic shapes. Some exapmples were demnstrated, Then main puzzle was given to them.

Test metrics

During test few metrics were captured for analysis :

1. Time taken by the player to complete puzzle correctly.
2. Attenstion percentage on reflective surface. A track was maintained for fraction of time player saw refelcdted image in surface.
3. Retention probabiity of player in continuing the game
4. Role of image to gather interest
